Jobs, dignity, and a connection — for Berliners in need.

What We Do

We connect people without homes or stable work with quick jobs in homes and local cafes — and we provide a phone and SIM card so they can stay connected.

Need a Handy + Work Offer a Job

How It Works

  1. Tell us what you need (or what help you can offer).
  2. We match helpers with jobs — fast and local.
  3. Everyone gets a phone to stay in the loop.
